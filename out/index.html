Who CoGen.energy is

We are a digital company reinventing access to clean energy.

We offer a platform that creates opportunities for energy consumers and other stakeholders to participate and benefit from the clean energy transition.

Co-owning and Co-generating

Join the energy transition whether you have a roof property or not through our co-generation and co-ownership packages.

Accredited solar solution providers

Have access to accredited solar solution providers based on your estimated system size at shorter turnaround time.

Online monitoring of solar rooftop system

Know the generation performance and energy savings of your system anytime and anywhere through an online monitoring app.
